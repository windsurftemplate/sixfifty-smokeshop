# Six Fifty Homepage Redesign
Date: 2026-03-08

## Approach
Split Screen (Option 2) — gritty & bold, high contrast, raw textures, big typography.
Images: real photos for hero/categories, SVG line art for product cards.

## Sections

### Hero
- Full viewport, vertical split
- Left: dark `#0d0d0d` + grain texture, Bebas Neue ~8rem headline, tagline, 2 CTAs
- Right: `hero-bg.jpg` full-bleed with left-side dark gradient
- Thin gold vertical rule dividing halves

### Categories
- 3 tall cards (bongs, pipes, grinders category photos)
- Heavy dark gradient over bottom 60% of each photo
- Giant white category name overlay, description, gold outlined CTA
- Hover: photo zoom + brighten

### Best Sellers
- 4-column product grid, SVG art on dark surface
- Badges: Best Seller / New
- Gold price, Add to Cart button

### Trust Bar
- Full-width dark stripe, 4 columns, gold icons
- Discreet Shipping · Quality Curated · Local Roots · Free Returns

### About / Story
- Two-column split
- Left: giant outlined `650` + animated rings
- Right: bold headline + copy + CTA

### Newsletter
- Full-bleed dark, oversized headline `JOIN THE 650 CLUB`
- Inline email form, gold submit button

### Footer
- 4 columns: brand+social, shop, help, about
- Gold top border, legal disclaimer
